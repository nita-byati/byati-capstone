My Contributions

UX/UI Design Process

Working alongside Warren Goodell, I helped create an intuitive, accessible, and visually engaging user experience for our project.

01

User Research & Personas

I conducted user interviews and surveys to understand our target audience, creating detailed personas that guided our design decisions. This research revealed key pain points and opportunities that shaped our approach.

02

Information Architecture & Wireframes

I developed the site structure and user flows, creating low-fidelity wireframes that established the foundation for our interface design. This involved multiple iterations based on team feedback and user testing.

03

UI Design System

I created a comprehensive design system including typography, color palette, component library, and interaction patterns. This system ensured visual consistency while accommodating accessibility requirements.

04

Prototyping & User Testing

I developed interactive prototypes in Figma and conducted usability testing sessions, analyzing feedback to refine the design. This iterative process helped identify and resolve usability issues before development.

Marketing Strategy & Materials

I developed and implemented marketing strategies to effectively promote our project, creating cohesive brand materials and communication channels.

01

Brand Identity Development

I created a distinctive brand identity including logo, color scheme, typography, and voice guidelines. This consistent visual language strengthened brand recognition across all marketing touchpoints.

02

Marketing Collateral

I designed promotional materials including digital assets, presentation decks, and social media content. These materials effectively communicated our project's value proposition and core features.

03

Campaign Strategy

I developed a multi-channel marketing strategy with targeted messaging for different audience segments. This included content planning, release scheduling, and performance metrics to measure effectiveness.
